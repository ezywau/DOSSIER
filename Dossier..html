THE PRAGUE ASSASSINATION
14 October 2018

TARGET

Name: Viktor Saranov

Description: Russian-Belarusian Oligarch

INCIDENT REPORT

At 08:49 local time, Saranov was killed inside a moving armored convoy crossing Prague. The projectile entered through reinforced ballistic glass and exited through the rear headrest. No gunshot was heard.

Forensic reconstruction later identified the firing point: an abandoned administrative building approximately 1.2 kilometers away. Counter-sniper teams reached the structure nine minutes later. They found no fingerprints, no shell casings, no rifle.

"You looked the wrong direction."— Handwritten note recovered beneath a rusted toolbox

THE DISAPPEARANCE YEARS — 2014–2017

No confirmed activity prior to Prague. Intelligence fragments place the subject in Serbia, Grozny, Benghazi, Aleppo, and Tangier.

This period is believed to mark the creation of the "Jackal" identity. Subject abandoned ordinary criminal structures. Instead of joining organizations, he constructed networks: forged passport suppliers, offshore banking facilitators, black-market engineers, ex-intelligence fixers, and private couriers.

No messages. No symbols. No signatures. Only impossible kills.

THE BERLIN ASSASSINATION
22 September 2023

TARGET

Name: Chancellor Matthias Krüger

Position: German Chancellor

Event: Berlin Economic Unity Summit

INFILTRATION

Subject infiltrated summit planning operations for six months under the Corvin identity. He assisted directly in stage sightline calibration, acoustic mapping, lighting placement, and security blind spot consultation.

He helped design the room from which there was no safe position.

INCIDENT REPORT

At 09:42 local time, Chancellor Krüger was struck while exiting his vehicle and entering the Berlin Economic Unity Summit. Witnesses initially believed the Chancellor had stumbled during movement between security corridors. No gunshot was heard.

Medical personnel on-site reported catastrophic neurological trauma inconsistent with visible external injury. Official cause of death: cerebral aneurysm. True cause: a precision ballistic projectile.

Forensic reconstruction later identified the firing point: an abandoned office building over 6 kilometers from the summit perimeter. Weather conditions at the time included variable crosswinds, intermittent rainfall, and unstable atmospheric drift. Multiple analysts concluded the shot should have been impossible.

Counter-sniper units searched the structure eleven minutes later. They found no rifle, no fingerprints, no shell casings, and evidence of recently dismantled surveillance equipment. One window overlooking the summit route had been carefully cleaned from the inside.

Projectile fragments evaded standard autopsy procedures. Subject crossed into Poland before the Chancellor was declared dead.
